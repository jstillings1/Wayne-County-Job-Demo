Commissioners

The seven member Board of Commissioners is responsible for considering and providing for the needs of the residents of the county. Commissioners are elected by districts except for At-Large and are elected every four years during the presidential election.

Board of Commissioners Meet

  • 1st and 3rd Tuesday of each month.
  • Briefing begins at 8 A.M. Meeting begins at 9 A.M.
  • All meetings are open to the public.
  • Join our LIVE BROADCAST during these times.
